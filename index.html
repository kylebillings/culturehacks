Discover CultureHacks

Founded to elevate, enrich, and solve for global challenges facing culture.

The first thing people do when visiting a new country or foreign community is eat their food, listen to their music, see their art, read their literature, engage in their traditions, and visit their institutions. Culture grounds the decisions we make and dictates our future, yet our education systems and governments show exponential decrease in value for culture. Businesses have an opportunity to shift this paradigm and create universal value for all cultural stakeholders.
